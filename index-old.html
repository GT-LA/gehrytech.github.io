Clients

GT helps companies across AEC improve the way they work including:

Owners - Our mission is to save owners time and money, and lower risk, while maximizing the value of project delivered. Our clients include some of the most visible names in the global economy, including developers, owner operators, and hospitality and retail brands. We believe projects work best when owners have visibility and control.

Services

GT offers a full spectrum of strategic and tactical services, designed to enable client teams to achieve the unprecedented in design, construction and facility operations including:

Project Delivery Systems - GT works with project owners, design teams and builders to develop custom project delivery systems that streamline the flow of information from conception to execution and operation

Design Optimization - GT design optimization services offer owners, architects and fabricators with solutions for the improved management of complex geometric and material challenges.

Design & Engineering Systems - GT design & engineering systems help many of the world’s leading architects and engineers to do what they do faster, better, with more control

Fabrication Systems - Our Fabrication Systems teams can work directly with your team throughout the entire project life cycle, from preconstruction services and tendering to shop drawing submission, shop floor fabrication and installation.

Construction Site Services - GT field teams have helped deliver world-class projects in many of the world’s most challenging construction environments.

Modeling Automation - Significant efficiencies and built-in quality result from the integration of repeatable production processes, custom computer scripts and advanced computing hardware.

4D Modeling- We can help you go far beyond traditional 4D schedule integration to help bring extraordinary precision, accuracy and visibility to project planning.

Project Standards & Planning - GT develops implementation plans that are systematic, ensure lifecycle value and ROI for the use of BIM on any project.

Facility Management - 3D viewing integration enables interactive management of the building systems in the context of the building.

Training - We offer a wide range of instructor-led and on the ground training in both technologies and project applications.
