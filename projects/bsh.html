Project Details

Client: Forest City Ratner

Location: Lower Manhattan, New York, NY

Type: Tower

Size: 1,000,000 sf

Team: Gehry Partners, WSP Cantor, Seinuk, JB&B

Description:

NY by Gehry, is a seventy-five story mixed-use tower, which houses a New York City Public School, office space for the New York Downtown Hospital, and over nine hundred residential units.

Highlights:

  • Improved the design process, enabling faster design analysis, price iteration, and construction optimization
  • Embedded construction knowledge and provided better costing information throughout the project lifecycle
  • Automated design to fabrication
  • Accelerated collaboration, create accountability, and optimize design
