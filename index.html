About

Legacy Gehry Technologies now Trimble Consulting Services grew out of the pioneering efforts of Frank Gehry and his team at Gehry Partners to adapt methods derived from the aerospace and automotive industries to complex projects of architecture, engineering and construction.

Mission We help our client to achieve unprecedented challenging projects while realizing tight control of budgets, schedules and quality by promoting working in a shared 3D digital environment and collaborating across disciplines to bring fabrication expertise and advanced technology adoption into the design and the construction process.

Vision We provide advanced project delivery services, BIM technology and Web-based 3D project collaboration to leading owners, architects, engineers, builders, fabricators and industry professionals worldwide, allowing customers of all sizes to communicate more effectively, improve design and construction quality and accelerate project cycle times.

Objectives Enabled by BIM and collaboration technology : 15-20% reduction to overall project costs, 1,000x increase in Data's frequency exchange, 50% reduction in unique building components, 300% acceleration of engineering work, 90% reduction in cost estimating effort
