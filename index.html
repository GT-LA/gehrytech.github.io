About

Legacy | Trimble Consulting Services grew out of the pioneering efforts of Frank Gehry and his team at Gehry Partners to adapt methods derived from the aerospace and automotive industries to complex projects in architecture, engineering and construction.

Mission | We help our clients to achieve unprecedented results on challenging design, construction and operations projects while realizing a high level of control over budgets, schedules and quality through empowering, engaging and inspiring high performance teams.

Vision | We approach every project with the client’s goals and objectives in mind and achieve transformational results through a proven method of defining, designing, implementing and optimizing data-driven project systems. For a truly Constructible process we utilize BIM and shared 3D digital environments as a basis for project delivery.

Objectives | Enabled by advanced technologies, process engineering and expert consultants: 15-20% reduction to overall project costs, 1,000x increase in frequency of Data exchange, 50% reduction in unique building components, 300% acceleration of engineering work, 90% reduction in cost estimating effort.
